# drumkit

> Low-latency TUI MIDI drum sampler for electronic drum kits on Linux, macOS, and Windows

- **Sub-6ms latency** — lock-free audio pipeline
- **Drop-in samples** — name WAV files by MIDI note number and play
- **Velocity layers & round-robin** — dynamic, realistic expression
- **Built-in Kit Store** — browse and download kits instantly
